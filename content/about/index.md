Northern Illinois University. I spend a lot of my free time running an 
extensive homelab — Proxmox, TrueNAS, UniFi networking, self-hosted media,

than I could have learned any other way. My GitHub has projects ranging 
from a RISC-V CPU simulator in C++ to work across Python, Java, and JavaScript.

Professionally, I'm focused on breaking into software engineering, data 
engineering, and DevOps. I'm actively sharpening my skills in SQL, PySpark, 
and Python. One of my proudest moments was leading CrowdStrike incident 
recovery at Advocate Condell Medical Center — my team was the first in the 
region to get fully back online.

Outside of tech, I'm into Warhammer, D&D, 3D printing, and DIY home projects 
covering everything from electrical to plumbing. I'm also bilingual in English 
and Spanish.
